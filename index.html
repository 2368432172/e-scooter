About Us

A bunch of mates who'd rather be outside.

We started Electric Rides because we believe the best way to experience New Zealand is on two wheels — just with a bit of extra push.

Our Story

Friends first. Business second.

We're a group of mates from Auckland who share a borderline obsessive love for the outdoors. Surfing, mountain biking, trail running, camping — if it involves fresh air and sore legs, we're in. A couple of years ago one of us picked up an e-bike on a whim, and honestly, it changed everything. Suddenly the commute to work was something to look forward to. Weekend rides got longer. Hills stopped being a dealbreaker.

We got hooked — and we wanted to make that feeling accessible to everyone, not just people willing to drop five grand on a European brand. So we started Electric Rides in 2023.

Three years in, we've sold over 200 rides across New Zealand and learned a lot along the way. We test every model ourselves — on Auckland's hills, Wellington's wind, Christchurch's flats, and whatever the Coromandel throws at us. If a ride can't handle real NZ conditions, it doesn't make our range.

We're not a faceless import operation. We're real people who answer the phone, meet you for a pickup in central Auckland, and genuinely care that your ride works as well on month six as it did on day one. When you buy from us, you're supporting a small Kiwi crew doing something we love.

Good to Know

Shipping, returns & contact.

Shipping & Pickup

We ship nationwide across New Zealand. Most orders arrive within 5–10 business days depending on your location.

Prefer to collect in person? We offer free pickup from central Auckland — just select the option at checkout and we'll arrange a time that works.

Returns

Changed your mind? No worries. We accept returns on unused, undamaged products within 7 days of delivery. Items must be in original packaging and in the same condition you received them.

If your ride arrives damaged or faulty, get in touch straight away and we'll sort it out — no questions asked.
